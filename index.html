Privacy Policy

Hungry Labs Studio — Last updated: March 5, 2026

Hungry Labs Studio ("we", "us", or "our") develops and publishes free, ad-supported mobile games and applications. This Privacy Policy applies to all apps and games published by Hungry Labs Studio. It explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use any of our apps.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Our apps do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

1.1 Data Stored Locally

Our apps may store the following data only on your device using local storage:

  • Game progress (levels completed, scores, achievements)
  • User preferences (volume, visual settings, themes)
  • Virtual currency balances and in-game item inventory
  • Gameplay statistics and session history

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

1.2 Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We may use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data that helps us improve our apps. This may include:

  • App opens and session duration
  • General feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Device type, operating system version, and language

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It cannot be used to identify you personally. For more information, see the Firebase Privacy Policy.

1.3 Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our apps may use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (device identifier for ads)
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • Device and app interaction data

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Information from Apps.

2. How We Use Information

The limited data described above is used exclusively to:

  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Understand general app usage patterns to improve the experience
  • Display advertisements to support free access to our apps

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services that collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob

4. Local Notifications

Some of our apps may send local notifications (such as daily reminders or bonus alerts) if you grant permission. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication. You can disable them at any time through your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the app

Analytics data collected by Firebase is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

7. Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
